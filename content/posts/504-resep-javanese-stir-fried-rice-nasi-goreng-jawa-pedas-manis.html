Beberapa Bahan Yang digunakan untuk pembuatan Javanese stir-fried rice (nasi goreng jawa pedas manis):

  1. portions of cooked rice ~ 3
  2. cloves garlic, crushed ~ 4
  3. union (in UK union is pretty big) ~ 1
  4. kemiri nuts (candlenuts) ~ 2
  5. of bird's eye chili ~ 6 pieces
  6. Vegetables : white cabbage, union leaves ~
  7. eggs, beaten ~ 2
  8. Vegetable oil ~
  9. Sweet soy sauce ~
  10. Salt ~
  11. Seasoning ~
  12. To serve : Cucumber and tomato (optional) ~

Tata Cara Membuat Javanese stir-fried rice (nasi goreng jawa pedas manis):

  • Put the ingredients : mix garlic, union, kemiri nuts, salt in a mortal and pestle woods or stone, crush until smooth
  • Add 3 tbsp of vegetables oil in a wok, put all ingredients and fry in a medium heat until fragrance
  • Add vegetables into the wok, stir briefly (you can add beaten eggs) for couple minutes.
  • Drop the cooked rice, stir-fry for 3 minutes, add sweet soy sauce and toss together
  • Spoon the nasi goreng onto warmed plate. Overlap cucumber, tomato on the side of each plate (optional).
